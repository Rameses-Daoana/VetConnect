What is VetConnect?

VetConnect is a prototype web-based system designed to help veterinary clinics improve customer engagement and appointment management. It provides pet owners with a simple way to book appointments, access FAQs, and stay informed about clinic services.

Vision

To create a modern and accessible platform that bridges pet owners and veterinary clinic.

Mission

To streamline appointment scheduling and improve customer experience in veterinary clinics.

Services Offered

Consultation

Book a session with a licensed veterinarian.

Vaccination

Ensure your pets are protected and healthy.

Grooming

Professional care to keep pets clean and comfortable.

Emergency Care

Quick access to urgent veterinary attention.
