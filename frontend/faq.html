Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, appointments, and how VetConnect works. If you don’t see what you’re looking for, feel free to reach out to us directly.

Q. How do I book an appointment?

A. Simply go to our Appointment page, choose your preferred date and time, and confirm booking.

Q. Do I need an account to book?

A. Yes, creating a free account helps you manage and track your appointments easily.

Q. Can I reschedule or cancel an appointment?

A. Yes, you can log in to your dashboard and update your booking anytime.

Q. What services does VetConnect offer?

A. VetConnect supports general consultations, vaccinations, check-ups, and more.
